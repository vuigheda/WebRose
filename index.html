Best when it's four of us

Park Chaeyoung, better known by her English name Roseanne Park (born February 11, 1997 in Auckland, New Zealand) or Rosé, is the main vocalist and lead dancer of South Korean girl group BLACKPINK. Rosé also has a successful independent career, amassing a large fan following for herself as a soloist. She is best known as a soloist for her unique vocal timbre, largely receiving praise for its distinctness following her debut. As a solo singer, she has collaborated with labelmate G-Dragon on his 2012 track "Without You", which peaked at number ten on the Gaon Music Chart.
